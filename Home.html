About me.

Hi, I'm Matthew Murillo and welcome to my website. I am currently a Pre-Doctoral Fellow at Yale's Tobin Center for Economics Policy worker under Lorenzo Caliendo. I graduated from Yale University with a B.A. in Economics and Mathematics (Distinction).  My interests lie somewhere at the intersection between international trade, climate economics, and urban economics. 

You can find my research here and CV here. You can reach me at matthew.murillo@yale.edu
